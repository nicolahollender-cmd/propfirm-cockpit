Features

Everything you need to trade the rules, not just the chart

Switchable Firm Rules

FTMO, Apex, Topstep, and The5ers presets — or build a custom rule set for any firm not listed.

Real-Time Risk Gauges

Profit target, daily loss, drawdown, and trading-day progress — all updating live as you log trades.
